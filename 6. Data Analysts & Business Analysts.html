Our Methodology

From Data Chaos to Clarity

A structured, iterative analytics methodology proven across 350+ projects — aligned with industry frameworks like CRISP-DM and Agile BI.

1

Business Problem Framing

We start with your business questions, not your data. Understanding the decision you need to make shapes the entire analytics approach from day one.

2

Data Audit & Source Mapping

Full inventory of your data sources — CRM, ERP, marketing tools, spreadsheets, APIs. We assess quality, completeness, and identify gaps before modelling.

3

Data Engineering & Prep

ETL pipeline construction, data cleaning, schema design, and warehouse setup. We build the reliable data foundation every great analysis depends on.

4

Analysis, Modelling & Insights

Statistical analysis, machine learning model development, and insight generation. We translate patterns into plain-English recommendations with clear business impact.

5

Dashboard Delivery & Handover

Live dashboards deployed and handed over with full documentation, team training, and a 30-day support window to ensure adoption and value realisation.
