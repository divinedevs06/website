Our Creative Process

How We Turn Ideas Into Art

A structured yet creatively free workflow that balances your vision with strategic design thinking.

1

Discovery & Brand Deep-Dive

We study your brand personality, audience, competitors, and industry aesthetics through a detailed questionnaire and kick-off call before touching any design software.

2

Moodboard & Direction

We curate 2–3 distinct visual directions as moodboards — different styles, colour moods, and typographic approaches — so you can guide us towards your preference.

3

Concept Creation

Full design concepts are crafted for your chosen direction. Every element — shape, colour, typeface, spacing — is intentional and aligned with your brand strategy.

4

Refinement & Revisions

You give consolidated feedback, we refine. We iterate until the design feels exactly right — no revision limits on our standard packages.

5

Final Delivery & Guidelines

Deliverables in all formats (AI, EPS, SVG, PNG, PDF, JPG) plus a brand guidelines PDF so your team can use the assets correctly across every touchpoint.

Everything clients typically ask before starting a graphic design project with us.

All our deliverables include both editable source files and export-ready formats. Typically you'll receive: AI (Adobe Illustrator native), EPS (universal vector), SVG (web-ready vector), PDF (print-ready), PNG (transparent background, high-res), and JPG. For print projects we also supply CMYK PDF with crop marks and bleed. For multi-page documents, InDesign packaged files (INDD + links) are included.
For brand identity and logo projects, we present 3 unique initial concepts. Once you select a direction, we refine it through as many revision rounds as needed — we don't cap revisions arbitrarily. For print collateral and social media graphics, we deliver 1–2 concepts with 3 revision rounds included. Additional concepts are available at a small surcharge.
